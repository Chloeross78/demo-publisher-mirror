WIN! Build your designer beauty bag

January 24th 2017 / 4 comments

**THIS COMPETITION IS NOW CLOSED** Pick your dream designer handbag, fill it with your favourite beauty products and be in with a chance to win the lot, worth up to £737*

**THIS COMPETITION IS NOW CLOSED**

Belt tightening and cutting back on extras almost comes with the territory in January. So we think, now that the end is in sight, it’s time to reward ourselves for good behaviour with a fun interactive giveaway. Get The Gloss has joined forces with the team at luxury online shopping destination, Avenue 32, to give one reader the chance to win one of this season's newest designer handbags filled with your choice of top beauty products.

The challenge is in the choosing. To win, first pick which of the four bags below is your perfect match. Will you fall in love with Instagram favourite, Manu Atelier’s signature box bag, or do you prefer street style regular Simon Miller’s mini bucket bag? Or perhaps you fancy exuding a little Parisian chic with Sonia Rykiel's silver studded backpack, or are looking to stand out from the crowd with Les Petits Joueurs' colourful Love Bag?

Next, click through the slides to choose four products to put in your bag. There’ll be one product for you to pick on each screen. Will you select Smith & Cult’s iconic nail polish, Lancôme's signature red lipstick, the Kiehl’s cult favourite all-purpose moisturiser or Oribe’s new illumination face-palette?

Fancy a chance to win a designer handbag of your choice filled with your own curated collection of beauty treats? Simply click through the choices below and input your email address at the end to enter.

About Get the Gloss

Get The Gloss is the online destination for expert health and beauty. We work with leading makeup artists, hair stylists, nutritionists, personal trainers, trichologists and wellness practitioners to bring our readers the expert view on news and trends in beauty and wellness, and create specialist guides to help you be your happiest, healthiest self.

Led by founders Susannah Taylor and Sarah Vine, our editorial team works with our favourite writers and industry insiders from Amelia Freer to Peta Bee and Imogen Edwards-Jones, and with some of the world's leading brands in beauty and wellness, from Chanel and Estée Lauder to Barrecore and Frame - to bring you all the information and inspiration you need for skincare, haircare, makeup, fitness and nutrition in one place.
