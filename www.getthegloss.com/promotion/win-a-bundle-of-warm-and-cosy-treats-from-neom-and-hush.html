Win a bundle of warm and cosy treats from Neom Organics and hush

… and snuggle up and relax with a copy of A Poem for Every Night of the Year

***THIS COMPETITION IS CLOSED***

The days are getting shorter and the nights longer and by now, many of us are looking forward to the evenings when we can put on some cosy pyjamas, fill the air with relaxing scents and curl up with a good book.

We have teamed up with Macmillan, the publishers of beautiful new poetry book, A Poem for Every Night of the Year, by Allie Esiri, to give away a hardback copy of the book together with a dreamily soft and cosy pair of Star Pyjamas, worth £49.50, from women’s clothing brand, hush, and a divine collection of sleep-inducing scented treats from fragrance brand, Neon Organics: a three-wick candle, a bottle of bath and shower drops and a pillow spray, altogether worth over £110.

A Poem for Every Night of the Year is an anthology of 366 poems by writers familiar and new. Each poem has a connection to a particular date – from annual celebrations, such as Diwali and Ramadam, to key moments and figures in history, from Rosa Parks (human rights activist), to Amelia Earhart (American aviation pioneer).

If tucking up with a good book in a pair of soft starry pyjamas, surrounded by relaxing, tranquil scents is your idea of a good night in, then don’t miss out!

Enter your details below for your chance to win by Sunday 2nd October 2016.
