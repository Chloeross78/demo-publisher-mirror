Ask Sarah Vine a Question

Log in to ask Sarah Vine a question

  • Martine Boogaerts asked: Hi Sarah, you have mentioned a few times that you suffer from adult acne occasionally. I am your age and have the same problem. Do you know if it is okay for adults to use Clearasil washes and creams? I do not mean the tinted stuff that is aimed at adults, I find it turns into cement and smells awful. I have used the washes and have had good results but people tell me it is not suitable for adults particularly if, like me, you use it regularly. Any thoughts or tips? thanks!

    March 1st 2015 11:10:08

    Sarah Vine replied: Hi Martine, I've found that Clearasil can be a bit harsh and dry out the skin. Have you tried Cetaphil? It's very gentle and cleanses thoroughly without aggravating acne. You can pick it up in Boots for around £7-£8- bottles are generously sized so it should last you ages. Hope that helps- do let me know!

    March 3rd 2015 09:54:52

About Get the Gloss

Get The Gloss is the online destination for expert health and beauty. We work with leading makeup artists, hair stylists, nutritionists, personal trainers, trichologists and wellness practitioners to bring our readers the expert view on news and trends in beauty and wellness, and create specialist guides to help you be your happiest, healthiest self.

Led by founders Susannah Taylor and Sarah Vine, our editorial team works with our favourite writers and industry insiders from Amelia Freer to Peta Bee and Imogen Edwards-Jones, and with some of the world's leading brands in beauty and wellness, from Chanel and Estée Lauder to Barrecore and Frame - to bring you all the information and inspiration you need for skincare, haircare, makeup, fitness and nutrition in one place.
