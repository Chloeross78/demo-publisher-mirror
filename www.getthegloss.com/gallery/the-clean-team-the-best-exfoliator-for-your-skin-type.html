The clean team: the best exfoliator for your skin type

An essential part of any girl’s skincare regime, the powers of good quality exfoliation cannot be underestimated. A vital step for maintaining a healthy skin barrier, boosting collagen production and ensuring optimum efficacy of your beauty products, we’ve tried a bevy of skin boosting power cleaners to help you in the hunt for the very best product for your skin type.

With a range of different textures and formulations available to suit a variety of different skin needs, now has never been a better time to take your cleansing routine to the next level. Whether you have dry skin, normal skin, combination skin, breakout-prone skin or oily skin, the key to smoother, clearer and softer skin could be closer than you think.

Follow us @getthegloss and Ayesha @ayesha_muttu.

2 / 12

Sensitive skin: Pai Kukui and Jojoba Bead Skin Brightening Exfoliator

If you have sensitive skin, we wouldn’t be surprised if the thought of exfoliators pretty much terrifies you. However worry not, as Pai’s organic products are the ones to beat for their gentle approach, and even more so when it comes to this face scrub.

The perfectly cylindrical beads aren’t harsh on the skin’s surface, giving a polishing rather than scrubbing effect to carefully lift dead skin away. As you massage it in, the formula develops into a silky, milk-like consistency, and it leaves complexions visibly brighter rather than red and distressed as other exfoliators tend to. It has a divine natural scent thanks to the oils too - a real treat and treatment in one.

£22. Buy online.

3 / 12

Sensitive to normal skin: REN Rosa Centifolia Gentle Exfoliating Cleanser

If you have normal skin which tends to err time to time on the more sensitive side, this is a great option. Comprising of a clever 2 in 1 formula, the wax microbeads provide a deep and thorough cleanse, before melting into the skin to leave it hydrated. Don’t be put off by its slightly larger particles - our testing left us with zero irritation and our complexions feeling incredibly soft the following morning, not an angry red patch in sight.

£15. Buy online.

4 / 12

Dry to normal skin: Malin & Goetz 10% Glycolic Acid Pads

For a quick dose of fast yet effective exfoliation, look no further than these easy to use skin perfecting pads. If you’re put off by products with the word ‘acid’ in the name, it’s completely understandable; you are putting them on your face after all. However, you needn’t be, as these pads are certain to change your perceptions of what an exfoliator looks and feels like. Skin chemistry of the most savvy, this acts as a great choice for when your epidermes are feeling a little dull and drab, leaving skin feeling squeaky clean, fresher and smoother to provide an ideal exfoliator alternative in the winter months when skin can feel particularly dry.  

£38. Buy online.

5 / 12

Dry to normal skin: Liz Earle Gentle Face Exfoliator

The perfect skin pick-me-up that works gently to smooth and brighten dry to normal skin types in particular, this face scrub is brilliant at buffing and reinvigorating a lacklustre complexion. With a combination of jojoba beads, eucalyptus oil, cocoa butter and sweet almond oil to cleanse, polish and hydrate in one fell swoop, it leaves skin soft, supple and refreshed to provide the perfect foundation for the rest of your skincare regime to build on.  

From £6.25. Buy online.

Dry to normal skin: Aurelia Probiotic Skincare Refine and Polish

A one-stop shop for beautifully soft skin, this is our pick for a luxurious treat if you’ve got a big occasion coming up. With a multitude of different uses, you can use it as an exfoliator, a peel or a mask thanks to its supremely flexible formulation. Enriched with BioOrganic and essential oils and natural rice brand beads to help boost circulation, it’s the milky cleanser/exfoliator hybrid that has fast become our extremely addictive night time ritual. Oh, and did we mention that it also smells amazing too? Our obsession continues to grow...

Combination skin: Elemis Papaya Enzyme Peel

A tropical dose of sunnier shores to perk up dull skin, this exfoliator containing natural fruit enzymes of papaya and pineapple provides the perfect way to brighten up stormy complexions. A cream textured wonder that helps moisturise, repair and protect as well as give pores a hard-working cleansing helping hand too, it also smells incredible to leave skin feeling wonderfully soft for days afterwards.  

£31. Buy online.

8 / 12

Combination skin: Indeed Labs Facial Powdered Exfoliator

A clever water-activated powder exfoliator, think of this pick as a power cleaner for your skin. Skin is left feeling softer than ever - almost as if it’s able to breathe again. With bamboo extract to gently cleanse, rice bran powder to re-balance, Okinawa red algae to strengthen, hyaluronic acid to hydrate and bromelain enzymes to remove dead skin cells, it covers all bases when it comes to boosting skin on all fronts. As it’s a powder formula, it takes a bit of practice to make sure you’re using the right amount but stick with it - the results are honestly worth the initial stage of trial and error.

£18.99. Buy online.

Normal, combination and oily skin: La Roche-Posay Physiological Ultra-fine Cleansing Scrub

Non-abrasive and soothing, this super soft exfoliator offers the best of both worlds - gentle cleansing and deeply refreshing results. Ideal for a range of different skin types, it proves that a scrub needn’t be too rigorous in order to obtain optimum results. Non-comedogenic and wonderfully good value, skin’s left feeling softer, smoother and clearer with blemishes noticeably reduced.

£10. Buy online.

10 / 12

Breakout prone and oily skin: Vichy Normaderm 3 in 1 Cleanser

A cleanser, scrub and mask in one, this product not only saves pocket money and precious bathroom space, but also oily skin. Creamy and non-abrasive, it's soothing and softening, bringing down redness effectively and leaving skin feeling hydrated yet matte. It feels very gentle and is safe for regular use, which is refreshing for a product aimed at acne prone skins (some are eye-wateringly strong). As a mask it gets to work in just 5 minutes. Did we mention it's just £11? A very sound skincare investment if you ask us.

£11. Buy online.

Breakout prone and oily skin: Goldfaden MD Doctor's Scrub

Expensive but worth the pricetag, this skin perfector exfoliates skin like a dream without stripping away its valuable moisture. Leaving the complexion clean but also soft, it’s best for those with very oily and problematic skin types in particular.

Activated when mixed with water, it definitely goes some way in keeping potential breakouts at bay to help make the investment all the more worthwhile.

£65. Buy online.

Dry, normal, combination and oily skin: Clarins Gentle Refiner Exfoliating Cream With Microbeads

Rich, creamy and packed with soft microbeads, this effective yet gentle exfoliator is one of those rare finds which suits a bevy of different skin types. Satisfyingly deep cleansing without being scratchy in texture, it quickly and efficiently sets to work dissolving dead skin cells leaving skin noticeably glowing afterwards. Even after your first try, you’ll notice that your skin looks more radiant and feels slightly firmer too for a quick fix skin wake-up-call to kick it out of bed.
