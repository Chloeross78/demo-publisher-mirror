Mortar & Milk - Clinical Aesthetician

Mortar & Milk - Clinical Aesthetician

Employer Mortar & Milk

Posted January 4th 2017

Salary DOE

Industry Beauty

Contract Employed

Get the Gloss Reference: 8181

Visit website

Forward to a friend

Due to our quick growth, Mortar & Milk is looking for another clinical aesthetician to help treat our growing client list, either full time or part time.

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

  • Passion about skin, and skincare, is critical. It’s what we are 100% about, so this position is for that person who lives and breathes healthy skin
  • A deep understanding of skin on a science-based level (we are happy to train up for the right candidate)
  • Flexibility, compassion, and an ability to listen without judgment
  • Previous 2 years experience working within a professional and high standard spa environment
  • NVQ Level 3 in Beauty Therapy, or preferably Level 4 Clinical Aesthetics
  • Proven record of retail selling skills
  • Excellent customer service, communication, and interpersonal skills
  • Must have a strong working knowledge of the science of skin

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Work with each client to make sure they feel safe, and to treat each client effectively.
  • Ensure delivery of exceptional standards of customer care
  • Maintain the highest standard of visual presentation in the clinic
  • Continually raise awareness of the brand and educate clients in the product and treatment range

Send your CV and cover letter including why/how you got into skincare, a list of brands you’ve worked with and the best experience you’ve had with a client to info@mortarandmilk.com, remembering to include ‘Get The Job’ in the subject line.
