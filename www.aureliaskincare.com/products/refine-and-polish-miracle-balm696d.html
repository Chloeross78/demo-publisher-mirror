Product Details

Aurelia is proud to premier this revolutionary double-action, multi-use enzyme polish which works to transform and brighten dull, rough, congested and lacklustre complexions.

This unique product features our signature probiotic and peptide complexes to balance and protect the skin, while a medley of the purest skin-brightening BioOrganic oils emulsify on contact with water. The Miracle Balm is fragranced with our uplifting essential oil blend of Chamomile, Eucalyptus, Rosemary and Bergamot and contains natural rice bran refining beads to gently dislodge dry skin cells, boosting circulation through manual massage to reveal bright, clear and beautiful skin.

As a mask leave on the skin for 5 minutes allowing the powerful plant enzymes to activate and deeply exfoliate skin by targeting dead surface cells, transforming the foundation of your skin. On contact with water the balm will instantly turn into a milky fluid, rinsing completely clean. Skin is left silky soft, smooth and glowing. Read Testimonials

TO USE

AS AN ENZYME MASK:

Step 1: Apply the balm generously to clean, dry skin using dry fingers. Avoid the eye area.


Step 2: Leave on the skin for 5 minutes to allow the active fruit enzymes to deeply exfoliate. You will feel a slight warming sensation.


Step 3: Add a few drops of warm water to your face to transform this luxurious balm into a gentle milk. Massage in circular motions to boost circulation to the skin. Rinse off.


AS AN INSTANT EXFOLIATOR:

Step 1: After using Aurelia Miracle Cleanser, dry skin thoroughly and apply the Miracle Balm using dry fingers. Avoid the eye area.


Step 2: Massage in circular motions to gently dislodge dead skin cells and stimulate circulation. You will feel a slight warming sensation from the actives.


Step 3: Add a few drops of warm water to your face to transform this luxurious balm into a gentle milk. Rinse off.


PLEASE NOTE

This product is not housed in our recycled brown amber glass as the balm emulsifies when in contact with water.
