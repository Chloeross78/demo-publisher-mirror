Quench your thirst with a delicious green smoothie thats both refreshing and will keep you feeling healthy from the inside out.

Youll need:
Cucumber
Avocado
Apple
Lime
Kale

Method:
- Prepare your cucumber, avocado, apple, lime (to take the edge off the flavour of the greens) and kale (the most super green, leafy vegetable that there is)
- Put all of your ingredients into your Vitamix
- Add a couple of tea spoons of spirulina its a great way to give longer lasting energy and to pack in some more nutrients
- Top it up with Jax Coco water to provide the liquid and blend
