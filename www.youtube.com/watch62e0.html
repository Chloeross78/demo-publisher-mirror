In this video, Amelia shows us how we can get really creative with salads by using a few different ingredients that are still healthy.

You'll need:

Rocket
Pear
Red onion
Feta cheese
A lemon
Walnuts

Method:

- Cut some rocket and place into your bowl
- Peel a pear into your bowl
- Thinly slice half a red onion and sprinkle on top
- Thinly slice the feta cheese over the salad, or chop/rip apart as you wish
- Grate lemon zest over the salad
- Roughly chop walnuts and sprinkle them on your salad
- Season with salt, pepper and olive oil as you wish

And there you have it, a healthy salad that strays from the bland limp lettuce you might usually find in your lunchbox.
