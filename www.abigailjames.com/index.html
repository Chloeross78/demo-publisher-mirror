Products

Skincare products form the cornerstone of vibrant skin and what you use at home alongside the professional products used within my treatments. Nature is often powerful enough! Experience has taught me what works with the skin, that the combination of nature, science and cosmeceutical is the way to achieve visible and long-term results, It’s a combination that works - the results are visible.

Lifestyle

Balance. That’s what it’s all about, anything in excess is unhealthy. There’s no arguing with the wonderful and beautiful effects that a healthy diet and regular exercise can have on your body and skin. But I like to adopt a 70/30 approach. If you’re paying attention to your health and fitness most of the time, letting your hair down with a glass or two of wine or some of your favourite chocolate is good for your soul, and that can only be a great thing.

Technology

For a truly results driven approach I super-charge some of my treatments with an array of carefully selected, safe, advanced technology in the treatment room. Never something to be scared of, I know that the right equipment really can help to deliver amazing, long lasing results, without the need for more invasive procedures or compromising your health.

Treatments

It’s my hands-on, bespoke approach that I’m best known for. Bespoke is at the heart of my work and earned me the nickname “The queen of bespoke facials” with “magic hands” The blend of unique massage I have developed over the years combined with what I refer to as my artists pallet of products and tools, allows for a superior facial which will undoubtedly support your skin concerns.
I have proven, on my own skin and that of my loyal clients, that facials should not be seen as a luxury, if you want to achieve youthful vibrant skin they should be part of your routine. I also love to give you the skills and knowledge to take away to support your skin at home.

Nutrition

Good food means good skin. The skin is our largest organ and we need to keep it healthy by supporting it responsibly and providing it with the best building blocks. Food is one of the most powerful tools you have if you want vibrant, healthy skin. Feed it, and nourish it with the nutrition it needs and you’ll see the difference. I work with nutritionalists to provide a full 360 degree, inside-out approach for my clients.
