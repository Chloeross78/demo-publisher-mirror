    (With Elizabeth Arden Prevage City Smart SPF50)
    SPONSORED FEATURE

These pollutants and UV rays generate excess free radicals, which interfere with skin’s ability to retain moisture (as well its effectiveness at producing collagen and elastin - essential ingredients that help keep skin plump and healthy looking.) The result of this is accelerated ageing, as well as an increase in dark spots, inflammation, fine lines and wrinkles. Oh no!
