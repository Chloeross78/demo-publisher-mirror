A rich plant-based bath soap that takes a forceful exfoliating, yet hydrating approach to dry and dead skin cells. A combination of exfoliating ingredients together with nourishing oils that cleanses and stimulates cellular renewal, leaving the skin cleansed, radiant and with a renewed softend skin texture.

How to use:
Gently massage the skin with bar soap in circular motions to cleanse and help relax fatigued muscles. Please note that this soap is not formulated for use on face.
