The best bronzers for any skin tone and any skin type

From bronzers that enhance a tan to ones that help you contour like a pro, there’s a bevy of glow-getting products out there for any skin tone and skin type. Check out our pick of the best bronzers to help you find The One and make your skin look its very best in every light - no matter the forecast this summer.

Follow Ayesha on Twitter and Instagram.

2 / 15

Chanel Les Beiges

Perfect for

A healthy, subtle glow.

Skin tones

Fair to tanned.

Skin types

All.

The faux glow verdict

Sheer, matte and light in texture, expect this bronzer to offer both understated daytime wearability and just the right amount of coverage to make cheekbones pop. Best for those who have fairer complexions, it cleverly lifts tired-looking skin to give it a sophisticated pick-me-up and drape it in the healthiest of summer glows.  

£39. Buy online.

All.

The perfect back-of-the-cab beauty fix for providing maximum output with minimal input, these makeup multitaskers allow for greater precision and more targeted results. Simply swipe either Altai or Cappadoce on cheekbones and temples and blend with fingertips for a quick fire way to bolster your tan. Plus, it fits in your handbag too (it scores bonus points on that fact alone).

Makeup Revolution The One Blush Stick

Perfect for

A pop of bronzed colour that won’t break the bank

Skin tones

Medium to medium deep

Skin types

All

Faux glow verdict

The handy twist-up packaging of this particular bronzer makes touch ups on-the-go a much easier prospect and forgoes risking your favourite powdered bronzer ending up crumbled at the bottom of your handbag, (we’ve all been there).

Sleek and compact, application’s a doddle - simply draw/dab it on areas of the face you want to enhance and blend the creamy formula using your fingertips to eradicate any harsh lines. At £5, it really is a beauty bargain and it doesn’t compromise on quality, to create a healthy-glow-from-within look that could rival most of its more expensive counterparts.
